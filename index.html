The Origin Story

It all began in October 2025 during a mundane Computer Science class. To combat the tedium, Tigran Gabrielyan started experimenting with his rolling chair, discovering its engineering intricacies and the physics behind its movement. What started as teaching his friend Mark Grigoryan how to use the chair efficiently evolved into "Chair Physics" - initially a humorous mini-class conducted between lessons.

As word spread through the school and more students became interested in the unconventional lessons, the concept transformed from a five-minute break activity into something far more substantial. During a late-night call with Mark, Tigran proposed an ambitious idea that would change everything: create a motorized chair capable of 50 kph.

They brought in Yuchan Seo for his technical expertise in programming and systems engineering. Together, the three founders realized this wasn't just a joke anymore - it was a genuine engineering challenge that would teach them valuable skills in physics, programming, 3D design, and teamwork. What started as classroom boredom had evolved into a serious project with real engineering challenges to overcome.

Today, Chair Physics represents the intersection of curiosity, innovation, and collaborative engineering - proof that the best projects often start from the most unexpected places. The team continues to push boundaries, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application to create something truly unique.
