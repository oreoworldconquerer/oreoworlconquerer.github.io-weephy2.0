The Origin Story

It all began in October 2025 during a mundane Computer Science class. To combat the tedium, Tigran Gabrielyan started experimenting with the rolling chair he was sitting on, discovering its engineering intricacies. What started as teaching his friend Mark Grigoryan how to use the chair efficiently evolved into "Chair Physics" - initially a humorous mini-class between lessons.

As word spread and more students became interested, the concept transformed from a five-minute break activity into something more substantial. During a call with Mark, Tigran proposed an ambitious idea: create a motorized chair capable of 50 kph. They brought in Yuchan Seo for his technical expertise, and together, the three founders realized this wasn't just a joke anymore - it was a genuine engineering challenge that would teach them valuable skills in physics, programming, design, and teamwork.

Today, Chair Physics represents the intersection of curiosity, innovation, and collaborative engineering - proof that the best projects often start from the most unexpected places.
